## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Training Program

The training program is well organized with a clear learning path from basic to advanced topics, making it easy for beginners to start learning AWS. The lessons are structured logically and combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on laboratory exercises, allowing learners to gain practical experience without feeling overwhelmed.

### 2. Support from Mentors and the Management Team

Throughout the training and project implementation, the mentors as well as the AWS Study Group and FCAJ management team were always willing to answer questions related to the labs and internship project. Whenever I encountered technical issues, I was able to refer to the provided documentation or discuss them with the community to find appropriate solutions.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The program is highly relevant to my major in Software Engineering. The knowledge of Cloud Computing, AWS services, and Cloud-Native architecture has broadened my understanding beyond university coursework and provided valuable exposure to technologies widely used in the industry.

### 4. Learning and Development Opportunities

Through various labs and workshops, I gained practical experience with AWS services such as Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, IAM, VPC, Amazon RDS, AWS Lambda, and Amazon CloudWatch. More importantly, developing the **Cloud-Native AI Document Extractor & Analytics** project allowed me to understand how multiple AWS services can be integrated into a complete cloud solution.

### 5. Community and Learning Environment

The AWS Study Group and FCAJ communities provide a positive and collaborative learning environment where members actively share knowledge, documentation, and practical experience. This supportive atmosphere motivated me to continue learning and practicing throughout the internship.

### 6. Learning Materials and Workshops

The learning materials and video tutorials are comprehensive and easy to follow, making self-study much more effective. In addition, the workshops provided valuable insights into Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and career development in the technology industry.

## Personal Feedback

### What I Appreciated the Most

- A well-structured learning roadmap from fundamental to advanced AWS concepts.
- Hands-on experience through practical AWS laboratory exercises.
- Opportunities to attend workshops and interact with cloud professionals and industry experts.
- The chance to apply my knowledge to the **Cloud-Native AI Document Extractor & Analytics** internship project.

### Areas for Improvement

- Provide more hands-on labs focusing on AI services such as Amazon Bedrock and Amazon Textract, along with real-world deployment scenarios.
- Organize additional Q&A sessions or technical discussions between mentors and participants to facilitate knowledge sharing.

### Would I Recommend This Program?

Yes. I would confidently recommend this program to students and anyone interested in Cloud Computing and AWS. It offers a well-designed learning path, practical hands-on experience, and an active community that provides continuous support throughout the learning journey.

---

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

- Continue organizing more workshops covering the latest AWS technologies and best practices.
- Introduce additional real-world projects that allow participants to apply their technical knowledge in practical scenarios.
- Develop more hands-on labs focusing on Cloud-Native architecture, Serverless computing, and AI services on AWS.
- In the future, I hope to continue participating in AWS Study Group and FCAJ activities to further improve my cloud knowledge, gain more practical experience, and prepare for AWS certification exams.
