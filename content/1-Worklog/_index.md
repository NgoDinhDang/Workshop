During the **12-week internship** at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd.**, I participated in the training programs organized by **AWS Study Group** and **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)**. Throughout the internship, I learned the fundamental concepts of Cloud Computing, completed hands-on AWS Labs, and gradually applied the acquired knowledge to a practical cloud project.

The internship roadmap started with core AWS services such as **Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, IAM, Amazon VPC, Amazon RDS, AWS Lambda, and Amazon CloudWatch**. After completing the foundational training, I began developing the **Cloud-Native AI Document Extractor & Analytics** project, which integrates multiple AWS services following a serverless architecture. This internship helped me strengthen my technical knowledge, improve my ability to study technical documentation independently, gain practical experience in deploying cloud solutions, and better understand modern Cloud-Native architectures.
